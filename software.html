DiCon: Disease Control System

The Disease Control System DiCon provides a general optimization framework, with a special focus on distributed, parallel epidemic disease simulation with policy optimization.

Available here.

Texas Pandemic Flu Toolkit

The Texas Pandemic Flu Toolkit is a collection of tools developed by the University of Texas at Austin funded by the Texas Department of State Health Services (DSHS). These include tools for antiviral release scheduling and distribution, vaccine allocation and ventilator stockpiling, and simulating pandemic flu and public health responses.

Available here.

EpiFire

EpiFire is a fast C++ API and GUI for simulating the spread of epidemics on contact networks. It can be used to perform network-based percolation and chain-binomial simulations of susceptible-infected-recovered (SIR) disease transmission, as well as traditional non-network mass-action simulations.

API available here, and GUI available here.
